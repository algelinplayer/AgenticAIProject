FINAL PROJECT PLAN: EMAIL ROUTER
==================================================

As a Customer Support Representative, I want the Email Router system to automate responses to routine inquiries so that I can focus on addressing complex customer issues efficiently.

As an IT Administrator, I need the Email Router system to seamlessly integrate with our existing email services via SMTP, IMAP, and RESTful APIs to ensure smooth operation and minimal disruption to our current workflows.

As a Subject Matter Expert (SME), I expect the Email Router system to accurately route complex inquiries to me based on content analysis and defined business rules, allowing me to provide specialized assistance effectively.

As a Team Leader, I require the Email Router system to provide a management dashboard for monitoring system performance, workflow bottlenecks, and response metrics so that I can track the efficiency and effectiveness of our email management processes.

As a Compliance Officer, I seek assurance that the Email Router system complies with data protection regulations such as GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant standards to safeguard sensitive information and ensure legal compliance.

Grouping similar user stories related to email forwarding rules management can help define the features for this product. Some potential features could include:

1. Rule creation: Allow users to create custom email forwarding rules based on specific criteria such as sender, subject, or keywords.
2. Rule prioritization: Enable users to prioritize their email forwarding rules to ensure certain emails are forwarded before others.
3. Rule editing: Provide users with the ability to edit existing email forwarding rules to make adjustments as needed.
4. Rule activation/deactivation: Allow users to easily activate or deactivate email forwarding rules without having to delete them.
5. Rule monitoring: Provide users with visibility into which email forwarding rules are currently active and how they are impacting their email management.
6. Rule organization: Enable users to organize their email forwarding rules into folders or categories for better management and navigation.
7. Rule sharing: Allow users to share their email forwarding rules with others, such as team members or colleagues, for collaboration purposes.

Feature: Email Address Blocking
Description: This feature allows users to control the emails they receive by blocking specific email addresses or entire domains.
Key Functionality: 
1. Block specific email addresses to prevent unwanted emails.
2. Manage a list of blocked email addresses easily.
3. Receive notifications for emails from blocked addresses.
4. Temporarily unblock email addresses if needed.
5. Block entire domains to prevent emails from multiple addresses within that domain.
User Benefit: Users can effectively manage their incoming emails, reduce unwanted communication, and enhance their email security and privacy.
